## About
Analysis of retail store sales performance covering revenue distribution 
across channels, margin analysis by product category and country, 
and ABC classification to identify top-contributing products.

## Tools
Google Sheets (pivot tables, charts, ABC analysis)

## Dataset
Educational dataset — retail sales across Europe and Asia, multi-year

## Key Findings

**Product Categories**
- Highest revenue: Household, Cosmetics, Office Supplies
- Most popular by demand: Office Supplies, Beverages, Fruits
- Most profitable: Cosmetics
- Least profitable: Personal Care, Beverages, Fruits

**Geography**
- Present in 45 countries across Europe and Asia
- Top markets: Czech Republic, Ukraine, Bosnia and Herzegovina, 
  Macedonia, San Marino

**Seasonality & Operations**
- Peak order days: Friday through Sunday
- Seasonal demand spike for Vegetables and Snacks: March – September
- Average full cycle (order → delivery): 24 days
- Longest delivery: Cereals and Office Supplies (27 days)
- Shortest delivery: Personal Care (21 days)

## Recommendations
1. Develop year-round Snacks promotions to reduce seasonal dependency
2. Increase marketing spend on Household, Cosmetics, and Office Supplies
3. Revise pricing strategy across product lines
